Process for Requesting & Scheduling PTO

Process for Requesting & Scheduling PTO

Email Val and your Program Director(s) with the PTO request and cc: Christine DeNoon and Ariel Martins (she enters your requests into Hive).

Block off your work day(s) in Purple "Out of Office" so people can see you're not just blocked/busy but actually OOO.

Send a "[Your Name] OOO" calendar invite to the full PM team AND your working teams. Us the "all day event" checkbox vs. a 9a-5p AND mark it as "FREE" so it doesn't block people's calendars unnecessarily

Schedule a meeting to go over your coverage plan(s) with Val, your Program Director(s) and anyone else covering your projects.

If you will be out for more than 2 days, please prepare a word doc or excel sheet with the list of project items and notes for quick reference. For 1-2 day outages an email or Teams message could suffice pending whats being covered.
